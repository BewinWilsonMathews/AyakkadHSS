Souhrida Club


Adolescent counselling and Health Care Programme is a unique, novel and innovative programme conceived and launched by the Department of HigherSecondary Education. This programme is implemented in schools through Souhrida Clubs

The Souhrida Club initiated in 2014 assures a platform for the students to express their problems frankly. The aim is to improve physical, academic, social and interpersonal skills of students and lead them towards a successful adulthood. Adolescent counselling and health care programmes embodies the core essence of the club.
