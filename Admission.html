Admission to 11-12 classes

Eligibility Criteria
- Passed the Secondary School Leaving Certificate (SSLC) examination or equivalent.

Application Process
1. Online Application: Visit the official website at hscap.kerala.gov.in and apply online through the Single Window System (SWS).

Selection Procedure
- Merit-based selection determined by Class 10th/SSLC grades.
- No entrance exams for admission.

Required Documents
- SSLC mark sheet
- Other documents as specified in the prospectus

How to Apply
1. Visit hscap.kerala.gov.in
2. Download the Plus One admission prospectus
3. Read online registration instructions
4. Create candidate login-SWS
5. Fill and submit the application form

For the latest updates and notifications, keep an eye on the official website or check with the Directorate of Government Examinations, Kerala
