From the Principal's desk

Welcome to CA HSS Ayakkad. Our school is committed to providing quality education, nurturing talent, and fostering growth. We strive to create a supportive environment that empowers students to excel academically, creatively, and personally.

At CA HSS Ayakkad, we believe in holistic development, character building, and community service. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to ensure that every student receives the support and guidance they need to succeed. We believe in fostering a culture of curiosity, innovation, and inclusivity, where every individual feels valued and respected.

As we continue on our journey of excellence, I invite you to join us in our pursuit of academic distinction, personal growth, and community service. Together, let us create a brighter future for our students and our community.

From the Headmistress' desk

Our school , CAHSS Ayakkad is one of the Oldest and most traditional schools in Palakkad district of Kerala .Established in 1941, this school has educated and graduated thousands of students. Many of them live and work in various fields in different parts of the world. Our school has always tried to update itself by absorbing innovations while keeping the values and traditions of the past . Our goal is to make the best education available even to the most disadvantaged in the society and to develop students into good citizens with a sense of values through education . I write this note with great pride as the Headmistress of this institution. Now we are launching a new website for our school I wish this initiative all the success. I hope this website will be useful to our teachers, students, parents and the society as a whole.
