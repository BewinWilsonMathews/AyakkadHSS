Google Workspace

Google through its Google Workspace for Education provides our school with a suite of cloud-based tools for collaboration and productivity. These tools, including Gmail, Google Docs, Google Classroom, and more, aim to enhance teaching and learning experiences. Our school can utilize these tools to improve communication, streamline workflows, and foster a more collaborative learning environment. Every staff and student has an official email id under the domain ayakkadhss.edu.in

Adobe Creative Campus

Adobe Creative Campus is a program that provides free access to Adobe Express Premium for students and teachers. This program is designed to empower students in creative fields with the tools they need to learn and create. Adobe Express to offer a comprehensive Digital Creativity and AI Skills Program for students from grades 5 to 12. Adobe will conduct orientation classes, boot camps, creativity challenges and creativity clubs, desgined to align with the latest job markets and skill trends. This programme equips children with future-ready skills
